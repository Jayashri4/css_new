WHAT IS A FLOWER?

A flower, sometimes known as a bloom or blossom, is the reproductive structure found in flowering plants (plants of the division Angiospermae).

Flowers produce gametophytes, which in flowering plants consist of a few haploid cells which produce gametes. The "male" gametophyte, which produces non-motile sperm, is enclosed within pollen grains; the "female" gametophyte is contained within the ovule.

TYPES OF FLOWERS

There are three primary kinds of flowers:
  • Annuals
  • Perennials
  • Biennials
